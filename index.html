  • Product Registration Automation in ERP with Python

    A company needed to migrate 5,000 products from one ERP system to another, which would have taken months.
    I used Python and Selenium to automate capturing the fields from the product spreadsheet and entering them into the new ERP system. This process was completed in a few hours, saving months of repetitive work.

  • Automating Customer Load in the System using Power Automate

Then, each spreadsheet was converted from .xlsx to .csv and split into two parts. After these steps, the four resulting files were uploaded to the system to update the customers.
