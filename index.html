    Company Deployment Process in Salesforce with UiPath

    The manual process involved receiving a spreadsheet from the broker by email, downloading it, and manually entering the data into Salesforce.

  • Product Registration Automation in ERP with Python

    A company needed to migrate 5,000 products from one ERP system to another, which would have taken months.
    I used Python and Selenium to automate capturing the fields from the product spreadsheet and entering them into the new ERP system. This process was completed in a few hours, saving months of repetitive work.

  • Automating Customer Load in the System using Power Automate

    This task involved updating a customer spreadsheet connected to a database, creating two copies of the spreadsheet (one for additions and one for modifications), and modifying the column in the modification copy. Then, each spreadsheet was converted from .xlsx to .csv and split into two parts. After these steps, the four resulting files were uploaded to the system to update the customers.
